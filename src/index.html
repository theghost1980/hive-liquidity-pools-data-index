Hive Liquidity Pools Snapshots (2025)

Daily snapshots of Hive liquidity pools are available, organized by token pairs. Each folder follows the format: TOKEN1_TOKEN2. Inside, you'll find files named as ts_[unix_timestamp], representing 24h snapshots.
